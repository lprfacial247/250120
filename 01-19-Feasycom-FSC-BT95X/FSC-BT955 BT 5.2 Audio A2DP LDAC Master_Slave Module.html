FSC-BT955 BT 5.2 Audio A2DP LDAC Master/Slave Module

FSC-BT955 is a Bluetooth dual-mode module supporting Bluetooth Low Energy and BR/EDR compliant systems for audio and data communication. It integrates an ultra-low-power DSP and application processor with embedded flash memory, a high-performance stereo codec, a power management subsystem, I²S, LED drivers, and ADC I/O in a SoC IC. By default, the FSC-BT955 module is equipped with powerful and easy-to-use Feasycom firmware, which is simple and fully encapsulated. Feasycom firmware allows users to access Bluetooth functionality with simple ASCII commands delivered to the module over a serial interface, functioning just like a Bluetooth modem. Therefore, FSC-BT955 provides an ideal solution for developers looking to integrate Bluetooth wireless technology into their designs.
